Classic Cheesecake Recipe

🍴

Prep Time

45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 600g cream cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F)
  2. Mix biscuit crumbs with melted butter

Nutritional information per serving:

Calories 350kcal
